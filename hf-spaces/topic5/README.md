# Monetary Economics EC3014 - Topic 5 Questions

Interactive AI-powered tutorial for Topic 5: New Classical Macroeconomics. Answer questions on expectations formation, Lucas Aggregate Supply, monetary policy volatility, and systematic stabilisation policy with AI-powered feedback.

## Features

- 4 essay-style questions covering New Classical theory
- AI-powered feedback using Groq's Llama 3.3 70B model
- Mobile-responsive design
- Hints and guidance (not complete answers)

## Topics Covered

1. **Adaptive vs Rational Expectations**: Backward-looking vs forward-looking behaviour, information set, Lucas critique
2. **Lucas Aggregate Supply Equation**: Price surprises, Friedman's monetary policy analysis, expectations and output
3. **Monetary Policy Volatility**: Signal extraction, how volatility affects output responses, policy predictability
4. **Systematic Monetary Policy**: Information asymmetry, technology shocks, policy rules and stabilisation

## Usage

1. Read each question carefully
2. Type your answer in the text box
3. Click "Get AI Feedback" for hints and guidance
4. Revise and improve your understanding
5. Bring your work to tutorial for discussion with your human tutor

## Note

AI feedback is a starting point only. Always verify against lecture notes and discuss with your tutor.
