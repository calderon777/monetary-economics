# Monetary Economics EC3014 - Topic 4 Questions

Interactive AI-powered tutorial for Topic 4: Friedman's Monetarism. Answer questions on monetarist labour market equilibrium, money illusion, and dynamic monetary theory with AI-powered feedback.

## Features

- 3 questions covering Friedman's monetarist framework
- AI-powered feedback using Groq's Llama 3.3 70B model
- Mobile-responsive design
- Hints and guidance (not complete answers)

## Topics Covered

1. **Monetarist Labour Market Model**: Equilibrium determination, dichotomy of real/nominal variables, neutrality of money
2. **Money Illusion Effects**: Short-run monetary non-neutrality, inflation lag, Friedman's expectations hypothesis
3. **Dynamic Quantity Theory**: Monetary growth, inflation, Fisher equation, Phillips curve trade-offs

## Usage

1. Read each question carefully
2. Type your answer in the text box
3. Click "Get AI Feedback" for hints and guidance
4. Revise and improve your understanding
5. Bring your work to tutorial for discussion with your human tutor

## Note

AI feedback is a starting point only. Always verify against lecture notes and discuss with your tutor.
