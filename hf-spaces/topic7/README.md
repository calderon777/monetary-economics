# Monetary Economics EC3014 - Topic 7 Questions

Interactive AI-powered tutorial for Topic 7: Monetary Policy Instruments and Inflation Targeting. Answer questions on policy rates, transmission channels, and optimal inflation targets with AI-powered feedback.

## Features

- 3 essay-style questions on monetary policy instruments
- AI-powered feedback using Groq's Llama 3.3 70B model
- Mobile-responsive design
- Hints and guidance (not complete answers)

## Topics Covered

1. **Why Money Supply Targeting Failed**: Monetary base vs broader money, money multiplier instability, unstable money demand, financial innovation
2. **Optimal Inflation Target**: Deflation risk, zero lower bound, interest rate constraints, wage adjustment and labour market clearing
3. **Transmission Channels**: Policy rate, credit channel, expectations channel, exchange rate channel with interactive diagram

## Usage

1. Read each question carefully
2. Type your answer in the text box
3. Click "Get AI Feedback" for hints and guidance
4. Revise and improve your understanding
5. Bring your work to tutorial for discussion with your human tutor

## Note

AI feedback is a starting point only. Always verify against lecture notes and discuss with your tutor.
