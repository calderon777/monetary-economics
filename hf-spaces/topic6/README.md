# Monetary Economics EC3014 - Topic 6 Questions

Interactive AI-powered tutorial for Topic 6: Central Bank Credibility and Inflation Control. Answer questions on inflation bias, the CB loss function, and credible commitment mechanisms with AI-powered feedback.

## Features

- 2 essay-style questions on central bank credibility
- AI-powered feedback using Groq's Llama 3.3 70B model
- Mobile-responsive design
- Hints and guidance (not complete answers)

## Topics Covered

1. **Central Bank Loss Function and Inflation Bias**: Output targets, time-inconsistency, why accommodating inflation is rational, the inflation bias paradox
2. **Fixed Exchange Rates vs Alternative Credibility Mechanisms**: Exchange rate pegs, policy rules, central banker reputation, performance incentives

## Usage

1. Read each question carefully
2. Type your answer in the text box
3. Click "Get AI Feedback" for hints and guidance
4. Revise and improve your understanding
5. Bring your work to tutorial for discussion with your human tutor

## Note

AI feedback is a starting point only. Always verify against lecture notes and discuss with your tutor.
